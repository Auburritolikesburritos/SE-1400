Frogs

Charateristics of a frogs

  • Adaptable, frogs live on both water and land and they can adjust to new enviorment
  • Observant and quiet, frogs spend most there time observing, sitting still and watching
  • Relaxed, frogs are very chill and relaxed

This pet would suit you if...

  • People who need quiet animals, frogs dont make much noise and are primarily silent and observing
  • Someone who has a small apartment or house, frogs dont take up that much room
  • A peaceful home, one without kids, frogs need a stable quiet enviorment, frogs are very peaceful creatures

Costs of this pet

  • Food, $5 to $25 a month, the price depends on the species and size, you can get crickets, worms, roaches or fruit flies
  • Supplements, frog calcium and multivitamin dust about $1-$5 a month
  • Substrate replacement, $5-$15 a month, depends on the kind you get, coconut fiber, moss or bioactive top ups, some people replace monthly or every 2-3 months
  • Water and conditioning, frogs need dechlorinated water, $0 to $5 a month
  • Cleaning supplies, $1 to $4 a month
  • One time costs, enclosure, $30-$150, plants/decor, $10-$60

Frogs would not be ideal for someone...

  • Someone who doesnt want to maintain a humid enviorment
  • Someone who wants a snuggly pet, frogs are not ones to be affectionate
  • Someone who is uncomfortable with dealing with live insects, you would have to feed frogs live insects
  • Who cant provide the correct habitat for the frog

Fun facts about frogs

  • Frogs drink with their skin, most frogs absorb water through a special patch on their belly called the drinking path
  • Frogs can breathe through their skin too, they can absorb oxygen through, thats why they can take underwater naps
  • Some frogs can freeze and then come back to life, so they can basically freeze and then thaw out
  • Frogs were the first land animals with vocal cords
  • A group of frogs is called an army
