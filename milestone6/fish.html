Fish

Charateristics of a fish

  • Fish are quiet and calm, fish make no noise and create a peaceful environment
  • Fish can come in many different colors or patterns
  • Some fish can be friendly to other fish while others will kill the other fish in the tank
  • Fish are very sensitive pets, they can get stressed out very easily, water temperature changes or tank mates can stress them

This pet would suit you if...

  • Fish are a bit more low maintenance, they dont require walks, training or constant attention, feeding them and checking their tank is usually all you need to do
  • Fish are quiet, good for people who dont like noise, g
  • Fish are good for someone who has limited space, good for apartments or small living spaces
  • Fish are good for people who could be allergic to furry animals

Costs of this pet

  • Fish tanks, can range from $30 to $100 depeding on the size you get
  • Food, food costs $3-$10 per month, flake or pellet food last a while, food costs can also depend on how many fish you have
  • Water conditioner, $2-$6 a month, filter pads and cartridges need changing
  • Electricity, this covers aquarium lights, filter, heater and air pump, can cost $1-$10 a month
  • Testing kits, test kits last a long time $1-$5 a month, checking the PH or for ammonia

Fish would not be ideal for someone...

  • Wants a hands on or cuddly pet, fish can not be held or pet
  • Is unwilling to maintain a clean tank for the fish, neglecting to clean the tank, change the water, or clean the fliter can make them sick or even kill them
  • Cant commit to taking care of it for a long time, fish can live up to 10 years, most people look at fish as short time pets not expecting them to live as long as they do
  • Is young and might disturb the tank, fish are delicate and can be killed or stressed easily

Fun facts about fish

  • Fish can "sleep" with their eyes open, most fish actually dont have eyelids
  • Some fish can glow in the dark, Bioluminescent fish can produce their own light
  • Fish can recognize their owners, they can start to recognize who feeds them
  • Some fish change their color based on their moods, bettas and other tropical fish can show bright vibrant colors when happy or stressed
  • Fish do have personalities, some fish could be shy and some are bold
  • Fish can talk without sound, they communicate with how they move their body, color changes and through vibrations in the water
  • Some research has been done that shows some fish, like goldfish, can distinguish shapes and colors
  • Fish can be very social creatures, some fish can form social hierarchies,recognize tank mates, and experience stress when isolated
